# audyo
Music is such powerful thing, it can convey emotions and make other people feel things. I noticed that certain songs would hype me up for exams, while other songs left me thinking about life. Since I was a band kid and took a lot of music lessons, I have some fundamentals in music theory. I was wondering if it was chord progression, quantity of chords, or tempo that caused certain songs to have certain feelings to them. I found some Python tools that could extract chords, so I was going to make a tool where you pick a song, it extracts the chords and tempo, then based on predictions or user selection, the mood of the song is determined. But as priorities and interests shift, I unfortunately haven't gotten to implementing things. I made a rudimentry kivy implentation a while back, but it's probably not good.
